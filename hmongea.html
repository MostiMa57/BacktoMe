Job Fair

A job fair—sometimes called a career fair—is an event where employers and job seekers come together. Companies set up booths, and recruiters are available to talk to potential candidates. It's a great opportunity for people looking for jobs to network, learn about different companies, and even apply for positions on the spot.
Some job fairs focus on specific industries, like tech or healthcare, while others are broader, covering various fields. Universities also host career fairs to help students connect with employers.
